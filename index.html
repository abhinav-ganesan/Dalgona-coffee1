Dalgona Coffee Recipe

This yummy South Korean coffee treat that's all over the internet only requires four ingredients to make - all of which you probably have in your pantry - you'll feel like you've won the jackpot, right?

Ingredients

  • Instant Coffee Powder or Expresso Granules
  • Hot Water
  • Milk
  • Sugar

Dalgona Coffee Making Process

Step 1: Combine the instant coffee powder, sugar, and hot water
Add 2 tablespoons(tbsp) of instant coffee, 2 tbsp of sugar and 2 tbsp of hot water to a medium mixing bowl.
Step 2: Whisk, whisk, whisk until the coffee is light and fluffy!
Using an electric hand-held mixer, whip the coffee mixture until it is light brown, fluffy, and holds stiff peaks when the whisk is removed.
Step 3: Serve with Milk
Use a spoon to garnish a glass of hot or cold milk with the coffee cream. Serve with a straw and stir to enjoy!!!
